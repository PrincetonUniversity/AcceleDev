We are a chemical company specializing in the design, development, and production of multistep synthetic pathways to unique raw materials, intermediates, and active ingredients.

March 1 2003:

AcceleDev formally established in New Jersey, USA.

2003 - 2005:

Establishment of key customer and supplier partnerships had huge impact on company growth.

Dec 1, 2006:

AcceleDev Lianyungang, a new 33,000m2 China production site, completed construction and started operations.

March 1, 2007:

AcceleDev R&D Co Ltd. was formally established as an expansion of our China R&D capabilities.

2007:

Through its established partnerships, AcceleDev performed its first Pharmaceutical cGMP API FTE development program and also its first commercial API production campaign for a top 5 Innovator pharma company.

May 15, 2009:

AcceleDev R&D Co Ltd opened its new state of the art Nanjing research and development facility. The site officially started operations, specializing in the areas of process chemistry (research and optimization).

Nov 1, 2009:

China based pilot and commercial scale chemistry production operations expanded in Jiangsu (Haimen City).

May 15, 2010:

The highly successful launch and expansion of the Haimen production site, allowed for the profitable sale of AcceleDev Lianyungang.

May 1, 2011:

AcceleDev establishes Monmouth Junction, New Jersey, USA Laboratory in order to expand its support of Pharmaceutical medicinal chemistry and process innovation (design and optimization).

March 2012:

Seagate-AcceleDev Haimen receives audit and successful approval for making GMP-like pharmaceutical "Regulatory Starting Materials".

May 15, 2013:

Received commercial approval and primary supplier status for key 22-step regulatory starting material

January 1, 2014:

Our highly unique data management platform, serving mainly as a China sourcing and supplier qualification management tool, reached a milestone of having 25,000 unique molecules and 9,000 Chinese suppliers. This is a result of the team's daily dedication and effort towards establishing such a valuable and accurate tracking mechanism.

May 6, 2015:

Formal award ceremony held by Johnson&Johnson / Janssen, and AcceleDev is presented with Janssen's most prestigious annual award: "Research & Development Excellence Award". AcceleDev also receives 2nd award for "Supplier Excellence", in relation to our continued commitment to Environment Health and Safety, and continuous organization improvement.
