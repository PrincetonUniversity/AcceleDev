New Jersey Facility, United States

AcceleDev's New Jersey facility employs highly experienced PhD and BS researchers with tens of years of experience at major pharmaceutical companies. This team is primarily engaged in the design and development of innovative new multi-step chemistry routes, and troubleshooting, optimizing and scaling small scale Medchem procedures. This lab site is capable of rapid, sophisticated R&D work at the milligram scale, but also has the on-site tools necessary to optimize and produce at the multi-kilo scale. The scientists at our New Jersey facility are in constant communication with their counterparts in China, ensuring that their familitarity with the specialized needs of clients in the pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries benefit each project from start to finish. Thus we are always able to offer a broad range of various benefits in the areas of R&D and production capacity, technology-expertise, raw material sourcing and purchasing, logistics, seamless and efficient communication, and information exchange.

On-Site Equipment and Capabilities

  • 2,000 square feet of lab space
  • 5 stand-up fume hoods

  • Parr Shaker Hydrogenation Apparatus (50 psi)
