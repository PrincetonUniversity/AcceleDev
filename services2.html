AcceleDev is a world class R&D and production company, capable of taking a product all the way from idea to full-scale manufacturing and quality control. Whatever you need, our expert chemists and state of-the-art facilities are up to the task.

Process Design & Custom Synthesis

AcceleDev has over 100 R&D personnel with expertise in custom chemical synthesis. We have years of experiencs designing creative, multi-step routes to produce high yields of complicated and challenging molecules. Our partners gain exceptional value by combining our top-of-the-line process design expertise with out unparalleled pilot- and large-scale production capabilities.

Process Development

AcceleDev can optimize poor or unproven processes to control cost, quality, and risk. Our experienced R&D team can tailor chemical pathways for speed or scalability, and will work flexibly to meet a host of project requirements. Our expert process evaluation scientists assess each process to ensure that it will scale up efficiently and with high purity.

Pilot and Commercial Production

With multiple specialized facilities and over 800 personnel, AcceleDev is an able partner at any step of the prodction process. We have the equipment and expertise to work at the gram scale for optimization, the kilogram scale for pilot production, and the metric ton scale for full commercial production. Our experienced scientists fluently implement processes we design or optimize ourselves, and pathways that we recieve directly from the customer.

FTE Chemists

Our full-time equivalent chemists support our process design and development capabilities by acting as dedicated extensions of your workforce. These skilled AcceleDev personnel can readily develop, optimize, or learn your processes and transition the seamlessly into production at any scale. Using our FTE chemists limits your costs while giving you access to a highly-trained, secure workforce and ensuring the ironclad protection of your intellectual property.

Supply Chain Management

Ensuring that our customers recieve the best value,our expert supply chain management team has over 15 years of experience securing reliable access to high-quality raw materials. AcceleDev pre-audits all supplier factories and analyzes all products in our own labs before production. Our detailed records include 5,000 supplier organizations and 15,000 molecules, all of which are tracked for quality over time. Our unparalleled experience virtually eliminates risk and drastically shortens production lead times for our partners and customers.
