Chinese Labs and Facilities

With more than 1,000 cubic meters of reaction capacity and over 800 personnel, our overseas facilities are more than capable of meeting the requirements of a pilot or major manufacturing project. Over 100 of our personnel in China are experienced R&D scientists specializing in the development of novel synthetic pathways and optimizing existing routes for scale-up. All of our scientists are PhD and BS graduates with training from top Chinese and Western universities. Many of our personnel contract as dedicated full time equivalent chemists, representing our client's needs expertly on the ground and closely monitoring the project as it moves through the production pipeline. Our in-house analytical tools, and those of our six partner universities in China give us access to the best monitoring capabilities possible, including processes like low-temperature and high-pressure chromatography.

Each of our manufacturing facilities is equipped with pilot equipment and kilogram-scale production suites to ensure the safe and controlled scale-up of all chemical routes to commercial production. All sites operate under strict quality controls and enviromental health and safety standards in accordance with current manufacturing practice.
