New Jersey Facility, United States

AcceleDev's New Jersey facility employs PhD and BS researchers with years of experience at major pharmaceutical companies, available for FTE or project-based client work. This facility in predominantly responsible for our R&D and process development services, and is optimized for custom synthesis and scale-up from milligrams to kilograms. Critically, the scientists at our New Jersey facility are in constant communication with their counterparts in China, ensuring that their familitarity with the specialized needs of clients in the pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries benefit each project from start to finish.
