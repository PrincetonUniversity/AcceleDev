We operate and partner with multiple coordinated commercial production facilities in China, all staffed by expert chemists, analysts, logisticians, and production staff.

Chinese Production Facilities

Our full scale production operations in China are composed of four independent sites. These sites each include pilot and full scale commercial production operations, allowing us the ability to proceed and scale every process from mg to multi-MT, quickly and seamlessly. Each site is well established, with each employing between 150 and 400 personnel. Each is also situated in special Industrial parks, following all the local and national rules and regulations. Also, each has a unique set of chemistry and operational expertise, as well as a range of reactor sizes and processing capacity. As a whole, these closely tied operations complement each other and give our customers a robust network of capability. This affords us the advantage of being able to meet nearly any chemistry, cost, or regulatory requirement. Our business is comprised of ~70% new custom synthesis R&D and scale-up work, and ~30% routine production (kilo - MT scale). If you would like more detailed information about any of our sites, please ask. We welcome the opportunity to share this with you at any time.

Jiangsu SeaGate-AcceleDev Chemical Science Co. Ltd.

Address:
No.17 Daqing Road
Qinglonggang, Haimen, Jiangsu, China 226121

General Overview

This site (27,000m2) located on a local industrial park, started operations in 1995 and currently employs 180 people. In 2010, the site completed major upgrades according to western standards, in terms of environmental, health, safety and quality control. These high standards are based on the owners' dedication to the same, and are realized through the efforts of working closely with area industry experts and our customers' specialized teams. Now, we are especially proud that this ISO9001 and ISO14001 certified site has also achieved the high pharmaceutical standard of "Approved Regulator Starting Material" supplier, making this is one of our primary sites for managing sophisticated chemistry in an exceptional, highly-controlled environment.
