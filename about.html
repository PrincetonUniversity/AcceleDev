History

AcceleDev was founded in April 2003 by Charlie Lewis, a former research and development chemist at Bristol-Myers Squibb, and the Sigma-Aldrich Corporation. Since that time, Acceledev has built-up and further strengthened its comprehensive set of R&D, production and supply chain assets, located in both China and USA. This build-up includes both significant investment and key partnerships, which greatly strengthened the overall operations and broadened our capabilities. Some of these include AcceleDev R&D Co. Ltd in 2007, Seagate-AcceleDev production in 2010, and most recently our new USA research facility in 2011. In addition to these, we also continue to maintain our long term strategic integrated partnerships which give our customers an even greater degree of supply flexibility. Each of our sites has a unique set of chemistry and operations capabilities. These combine to give a comprehensive range of choices for our customers. Our expertise has continued to specialize in the design, development and production of multistep synthetic pathways to unique and diverse wide range of chemical molecules. Our multinational local US and China teams allow us to maximize all the advantages which can be gained from both regions. Our mission is simple: Wok cost efficiently, and provide high quality product on time, while also holding in highest regard our customer's need for responsive and regular communication.

What we do

AcceleDev offers a myriad of professional chemical services, including research and development, commercial production, and chemical supply. Our clients include major pharmaceutical companies, small biotechnology start-ups, and everyone in between. In keeping with our stellar record of service, our scientists can create or optimize reaction pathways to complicated molecules, and then produce them on any scale. We have an extensive and wide range of in-house chemistry technology, and the support of a strong, strategic raw material sourcing engine. These capabilities allow our customers' important programs to run more quickly and under far reduced risk. To learn more about our unique combination of services, please visit our services page.

Who we are

AcceleDev's chemists are PhD and BS scientists with decades of cumulative experience in the pharmaceutical industry, based both in the United States and China. Our sites are well equipped to support any new development program, and we also have well-positioned affiliations and partnerships that further expand our analytical and manufacturing capabilities, without increasing our low cost base.

What we stand for

The AcceleDev organization and core facilities operate at the highest level of industry standards. Our customers are premier pharmaceutical companies that expect and require this from their supply partners, and we are committed to exceeding these high standards in every project we undertake. In addition, our customers place their trust in us and in our ability to manage their programs and intellectual property effectively. We are bound by this trust and consider it to be among our most important duties. These are among our most important core operating principles.

Why we're unique

Native in the US. Native in China.

In addition to our expertise in chemistry R&D and production generally, we also have significant experience working closely with many different clients from the American and European pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries. Our scientists in the United States and China have over a decade of experience working together to complete these projects to specification and on time, so that no time is lost translating our clients' specialized needs from our Western lab to our production facilities in China. We were also among the first American companies to have its own analytical facilities in China, and no one has a more accurate and reliable supply chain for high-purity raw materials.

Every Step.

AcceleDev's scientists and facilities support the full chemical production pipeline, from process design and development to pilot and full-scale commercial production. We can help clients at any step, to devise or refine a synthesis, to scale up an existing process, and to manufacture and ship a commercial chemical product, helping you avoid the extra risk and time loss that come from multiple project transfers. With AcceleDev, you don't need any other supplier.

R&D Expertise.

Many of our scientists come from major pharmaceutical companies, and are well-aware of the needs and standards required to work with clients in that industry. Our team holds a broad range of chemistry training and experience from our roots in western pharmaceutical R&D, industrial pilot and commercial production, and academia.
