We are a chemical company specializing in the design, development, and production of multistep synthetic pathways to unique raw materials, intermediates, and active ingredients.

History

AcceleDev Chemical, LLC was founded in 2003 by Charlie Lewis, a former research and development scientist at Sigma Aldrich. For the next several years, the company distinguished inself in chemical supply chain management, forging key partnerships with customers and high-quality chemical suppliers in China. AcceleDev was among the first American chemical companies to establish partnerships with Chinese chemistry professionals to offer on-site analytical testing, a necessary feature still rare in the industry. The business soon established its own research and development capabilities, and Lewis founded AcceleDev R&D Co. Ltd. in 2006. In 2009, AcceleDev added a new facility in Haimen, China to complement its developmet expertise with the capacity for full-scale commercial production. Now, AcceleDev continues to grow, hiring western-trained chemists in both the U.S. and China and developing innovative processes from pilot- to large-scale chemical production.

What we do

AcceleDev offers a myriad of professional chemical services, including research and development, commercial production, and chemical supply. Our clients include major pharmaceutical companies, small biotechnology start-ups, and everyone in between. In keeping with our stellar record of service, our scientists can create of optimize reaction pathways to complicated molecules, and then produce them on any scale. We also sell a variety of readily-available chemicals, all of which are analyzed in our own labs before shipment.
